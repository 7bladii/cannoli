About Us

Our story begins in a small kitchen in Palermo, Sicily, where Nonna Maria perfected her cannoli recipe over generations. It's a recipe built on love, patience, and the finest local ingredients. Today, we bring that same tradition to your table. Every cannoli is hand-filled, just moments before it reaches you, ensuring a crispy shell and a rich, creamy filling. This isn't just a dessert; it's a piece of our family history.

How to Order

Ready for a taste of Sicily? You can build your own box of cannolis directly from our "Our Flavors" section. Please note: to ensure availability, all orders must be placed a minimum of 48 hours in advance. Delivery is available within a 10-mile radius of West Hollywood. and in-store pickup.
